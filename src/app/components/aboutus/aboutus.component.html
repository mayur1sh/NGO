Who we are?

Dreams Peace Foundation was registered under Indian Trust Act 1882 in 2019, since then our organization has been expanding continuously in terms of social development. It has worked for the promotion of sustainable, equitable and participatory development, social welfare and community-based justice. We have earned recognition as an institution(organization) from state government of different states of India and International organizations. We as an organization contributing in the field of education, health and child protection.
