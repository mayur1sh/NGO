Programs

Play For Peace

Play for Peace is a group of people united by a dream of peace for our children—today and tomorrow—and guided by our mission and core values. Each of our communities creates the interventions most relevant to their needs and connects with the global learning community for learning, inspiration, and support.

LSET (Life Skills Education for Transformation

LSET Program is based upon the core set of life skills as identified by WHO (1994). Includes Social skills, Thinking skills, Emotional skills. All skills are interrelated. The practice of these life skills leads to an increased sense of competence and positive changes in behaviour. The ultimate goal for children is greater confidence and self-esteem, a healthy, positive lifestyle, and the ability to become self-sustaining citizens of the world community.
